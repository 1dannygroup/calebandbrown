Why traditional institutions choose Caleb & Brown

Crypto markets aren't always smooth sailing. That's why we exist - to help your clients navigate complexity and risk, so they can capitalise on this transformative asset class and build a better future.

Our personalised broker service delivers a human touch, giving your clients the answers and support they need to cut through misinformation and the complicated mechanics of cryptocurrency.

Personalised broker service

A dedicated broker delivering customised service, available 24/7 whenever your client has a question or needs to shift their position.

Battle-tested security

Your client's assets are secured by Fireblocks with end-to-end custody and multi-layer protection.

Reduced risk through expert execution

All responsibility and accountability during trading is held by us, mitigating risk and ensuring the security of your client's funds.

Transparent, all-inclusive fees

A single fixed percentage-based fee that covers everything — no hidden costs or surprises.
