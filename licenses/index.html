IRREVERSIBLE TRANSACTIONS WARNING

WARNING: ONCE EXECUTED, VIRTUAL CURRENCY TRANSACTIONS CANNOT BE UNDONE, REVERSED, OR RECALLED. LOSSES DUE TO FRAUDULENT OR ACCIDENTAL TRANSACTIONS MAY NOT BE RECOVERABLE. VIRTUAL CURRENCY TRANSACTIONS ARE IRREVERSIBLE AND MAY BE USED BY PERSONS SEEKING TO DEFRAUD CUSTOMERS, INCLUDING PERSONS IMPERSONATING GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS, LAW ENFORCEMENT, OR OTHER AUTHORITY FIGURES.

Virtual currency transactions, once executed and recorded on the blockchain, are permanently irreversible. This means:

  • You cannot cancel a transaction after it has been executed and confirmed on the blockchain.
  • If you send virtual currency to an incorrect address or wallet, that currency may be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.
  • If you are the victim of fraud or deception and send virtual currency to a scammer, you may have no recourse to recover your funds.
  • Transactions cannot be undone even if you discover an error immediately after execution.
  • Caleb and Brown Limited cannot reverse, recall, or undo a virtual currency transaction once it has been broadcast to the blockchain network.

Customer Rights and Protections

(1) Liability for Unauthorized Virtual Currency Transactions

You are solely responsible for maintaining the security of your Account credentials, including your password, email access, and two-factor authentication settings. Subject to applicable law and the Terms of Business (including clauses 8 and 10), you may be liable for unauthorized transactions that occur as a result of your failure to maintain the security of your Account credentials or your failure to promptly notify us of unauthorized access.

(2) Right to Stop Payment of Preauthorized Virtual Currency Transfers

For certain preauthorized or recurring virtual currency transactions (such as dollar cost average Trades or other standing Trade orders as described in clauses 9.1(d) and 9.2(f)), you have the right to stop payment by canceling the preauthorized transaction prior to execution. To initiate a stop-payment order, you must contact your Broker or submit a cancellation request through the Portal (if such functionality is available) prior to the scheduled execution of the transaction. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to process your stop-payment request, but we cannot guarantee that we will be able to stop payment if the transaction has already been executed or is in the process of being executed at the time we receive your request. Please note that once a virtual currency transaction has been executed and broadcast to the blockchain, it cannot be stopped or reversed under any circumstances.

(3) Disclosure of Customer Information to Third Parties

We will not disclose information concerning your Account to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Where necessary to provide the Services to you, including to execute Trades, hold Assets, or facilitate deposits and withdrawals as described in clause 5(e) of the Terms of Business;
  • Where you have provided your consent to such disclosure;
  • Where required to comply with applicable AML/CFT Laws or other legal obligations as described in clause 7;
  • Where required by a court order, legal obligation, subpoena, or order by a government agency;
  • To our related bodies corporate, service providers, and agents who assist us in providing the Services, subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations; or
  • As otherwise described in our Privacy Policy.

(4) Customer Information That May Be Disclosed to Third Parties

When we disclose customer information to third parties in the circumstances described above, the information disclosed may include:

  • Your personal information, including name, address, date of birth, and identification documents, as required for AML/CFT compliance;
  • Your Account information, including transaction history, balances, and trading activity;
  • Information about specific transactions, including the type, amount, date, and destination of virtual currency transfers;
  • Information necessary to execute Trades or hold Assets on your behalf through third-party platforms, exchanges, or custodians; and
  • Any other information reasonably necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, or respond to lawful requests from government agencies or courts.
  • For more information about how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information, please review our Privacy Policy.

If we begin to offer virtual currency kiosk services in the future, you will have the right to receive a physical, printed receipt for any virtual currency transaction conducted through such a kiosk at the time of the transaction. Such receipt will include all transaction details and relevant warnings.

(6) Right to Consent to Changes in Rules or Policies

As described in clause 1(b) and (c) of the Terms of Business, we may revise the Terms of Business from time to time.
